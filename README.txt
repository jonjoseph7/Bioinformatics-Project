Team: Jonathan Joseph, Vedansh Parvathaneni, John Dinh

Scientific Question: What are the expression differences in the effects of rotenone exposure across different types of cells, different age groups, and different durations of exposure?

Data: https://www.refine.bio/experiments/SRP055573/rna-seq-of-zebrafish-brain-liver-and-skin-during-perturbation-with-rotenone-at-young-and-old-age

Description: The dataset consists of 68 samples taken from zebrafish brain, liver, and skin cells that were treated with Rotenone over a 3 and 8-week period. They are also separated into 2 age groups 12 and 36 months old.
